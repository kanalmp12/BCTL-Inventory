PLEASE MANUALLY BACKUP YOUR GOOGLE SHEET DATA:
1. Open your Google Sheet.
2. Go to File > Make a copy.
3. Name it 'Backup_Data_Before_Admin_Update'.
------------------------------------------------
PLEASE MANUALLY VERSION YOUR APPS SCRIPT:
1. Open Extensions > Apps Script.
2. Click 'Manage Deployments' or Project History.
3. Save the current state as a version.
